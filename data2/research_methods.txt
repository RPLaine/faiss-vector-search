# Research and Investigation

## Research Methods

### Desk Research
- Online databases and archives
- Academic journals and publications
- Government records and reports
- Historical documents
- Social media analysis
- News archives

### Field Research
- Site visits and observation
- Interviews with stakeholders
- Document collection
- Photography and video documentation
- Public meetings and hearings

### Data Journalism
- Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests
- Public records requests
- Database analysis
- Statistical interpretation
- Data visualization
- Spreadsheet investigation

## Investigation Strategies

### Following the Money
- Track financial transactions
- Review tax records
- Examine corporate filings
- Investigate property records
- Analyze campaign contributions
- Study budget documents

### Building a Timeline
- Chronological event mapping
- Identify gaps in the narrative
- Find inconsistencies
- Establish cause and effect
- Document development of story

### Source Development
- Identify key players
- Build trust gradually
- Protect confidential sources
- Cultivate insider sources
- Use social networks for connections

## Organization and Documentation

### Note-Taking System
- Date and time all notes
- Record source information
- Distinguish facts from opinions
- Note direct quotes verbatim
- Mark items requiring follow-up

### File Management
- Create project folders by topic
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Backup files regularly
- Maintain source contact lists
- Keep an investigation log

### Citation Tracking
- Record URLs and access dates
- Save PDFs of online sources
- Photograph documents
- Note publication details
- Maintain a bibliography

## Safety and Security

### Source Protection
- Use encrypted communication
- Meet in secure locations
- Avoid electronic trails
- Use anonymous tip lines
- Protect identifying details

### Digital Security
- Use VPNs for research
- Encrypt sensitive files
- Secure passwords and 2FA
- Be aware of metadata
- Use secure browsers

### Personal Safety
- Inform editors of risky assignments
- Have emergency contacts
- Know your legal rights
- Trust your instincts
- Consider hostile environment training
